Tourism

Though not many people live in Vatican City, it has a large tourist industry. Several attractions and historical sites bring many to the city-state. It is also often a place for religious pilgrimage. Tours of different popular museums and sites like the Sistine Chapel fuel a lot of this city's economy.

Famous Tourists: Katy Perry
Revenue From Tourism: $80 million/year
Number of Hotels: None. No one is allowed to sleep overnight
here except a few.
Number of Airports: 1 (a heliport)
Tourist Sites: Sistene Chapel, St. Peter's Basilica
