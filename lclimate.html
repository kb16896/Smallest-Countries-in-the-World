Climate

Liechtenstein is in the Alps, so it has some really fun weather. During the winters, it snows a lot and gets quite cold. The Summers are wet and not too hot. Out of all of the countries in Europe, it is the only one that the entire country is in the alps.


Seasons: All 4
Average temperature: 50.6 degrees
Rainfall per year: 39.3 in/year
Main Terrain: Rough Alpine
