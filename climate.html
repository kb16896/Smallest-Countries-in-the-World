Climate

Nauru has a beutiful weather year round, split into two seasons. The temperatures range from 78 to 89 degrees year round. Half the year they have a tropical climate, while the other half of the year the cilmate is dry. Often, during the tropical climate, it will suddenly begin to rain very hard. For this reason, the people who live there often carry rain coats and umbrellas. They have pituresque beaches with beutiful palm trees.
