Climate

While San Marino has something of a medditeranian climate, it is different from the countries around it. It has 4 seasons. In the winter, it snows, but it doesn't get really cold. The Summers are long and dry. Overall, it doesn't get a lot of precipitation.


Seasons: All 4
Average temperature: 48.6 degrees
Rainfall per year: 3.67 in/year
Main Terrain: Rugged Mountains
