Climate

The Marshall Islands have a nice tropical climate. They do get a lot of rain and natural disasters as a result of the rain. They actually have their own calander for the weather and season, with special names in their own language.


Seasons: Wet and Dry (Rak and Anonean)
Average temperature: 82.6 degrees
Rainfall per year: 133.85 in/year
Main Terrain: Coral Atols
